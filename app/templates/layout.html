Services

Room service

In-room dining available 24/7, offering a variety of meals, snacks, and beverages. Options for breakfast in bed, romantic dinners, or late-night cravings.

Housekeeping services

Daily cleaning, linen changes, and turndown services. Laundry and dry cleaning services for guests’ clothing, with same-day options.

Business center

Meeting rooms, conference halls, and a business lounge with Wi-Fi and office supplies. Fax, photocopying, and printing services for business travelers.

Dining options

On-site restaurants with various cuisines, from fine dining to casual options. Coffee shop or lounge for quick bites and beverages.

Event and banquet services

Banquet halls and event spaces for weddings, parties, and corporate events. Full catering services, audiovisual equipment, and event planning assistance.

Kid's club and baby sitting services

Supervised kids’ club with games, crafts, and activities. Babysitting services for parents wanting a night out.

Special Requests and Customization

Personalized services like room decoration for celebrations or surprise events. Options for dietary preferences, special room setups, or unique guest requests.

Complimentary Breakfast or Happy Hour

Buffet breakfast or continental breakfast included in room rates. Evening happy hour with drinks and light snacks for guests.

Business Plan

For guests who need more flexibility and premium services, our Business Plan offers a tailored experience designed for professionals and corporate travelers. Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability. Please contact the front desk to inquire.

. Is the lodge pet-friendly?

Yes, we welcome pets! We offer designated pet-friendly rooms and cabins, and there are pet amenities available upon request. A small cleaning fee applies. Please notify us in advance if you're bringing a pet.

Do you offer spa services?

Yes! Our spa offers a range of treatments including massages, facials, and body therapies. You can book treatments in advance or while at the lodge.
